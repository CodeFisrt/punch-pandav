My Journey from COVID-19 Crisis to Conservation

On September 7th, 2020, life took an unexpected turn my entire family tested positive for COVID-19. We were quarantined, and my father had to be hospitalized for 22 long and anxious days. It was one of the most challenging phases of our lives. Thankfully, with strength, prayers, and care, we all recovered. But that experience left a lasting impact on me especially a deep appreciation for something we often take for granted: oxygen. After my father’s recovery, I came across the hospital bill for medical oxygen—it was around ₹20,000. That number stayed with me. I made a quiet promise to myself: every year, I would invest at least ₹20,000 towards something that gives life naturally—trees.
