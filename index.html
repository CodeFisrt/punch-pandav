Save The Planet Plant The Trees

Planting trees improves air quality, reduces carbon dioxide, and supports biodiversity. Every tree planted is a step toward a greener, healthier, and more sustainable Earth.

🌱 Make A Movement, Join Us, Save Our Planet

Every step plants a seed of change. Be the spark for a greener future — join our movement to restore the Earth through community efforts, education, and action.

Research and Education

From a personal journey post-COVID to a public resource hub, our educational initiative empowers nature enthusiasts with seed calendars and native tree knowledge.

Partnership & Community

We unite people with nature through seed distribution and eco-connectivity across villages and cities, fostering lasting green communities.

Delegation & Advocation

With over 10,000 saplings raised and shared, our mission encourages environmental ownership and advocacy for future-ready green leaders.

🌿 The Story Behind Our Movement

After my family battled COVID-19, I realized the true value of oxygen. This led me to reconnect with nature by planting trees. Though an engineer by profession, I dived deep into learning about native tree species, collecting seeds, and creating a planting calendar. Over the years, I’ve distributed seeds to more than 50 groups, gathered rare varieties from sacred groves, and nurtured over 10,000 saplings. Our goal is to freely distribute these saplings this monsoon and inspire others to join this green revolution.
